FAQ

Got questions?

What is Rec Room Revival?›
A community-driven project to recreate the classic Rec Room experience. Built by fans, for fans.
Is this official?›
No — fan-made and not affiliated with Rec Room Inc. We're doing this for the love of the game.
How do I get into beta?›
You already applied when you entered the site! We review all applications and contact selected testers.
Is it free?›
100% free. This is a community project, not a commercial one.
When does beta launch?›
Join the Discord — that's where we announce first. We'll be in touch with selected testers directly.
